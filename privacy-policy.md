# Codafy Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

Codafy ("the app") is a client application that lets you play music from a Plex Media Server that you own and control.

We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

This policy explains what stays on your device and which services the app communicates with.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect nothing.

The developer has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no backend that receives your data.

The app does not create an account with us.

---

## 2. Data Stored on Your Device

To function properly, PlexMusic stores the following information locally on your device:

- Your Plex authentication token and session.
- Cached audio, artwork, and lyrics.
- Music you choose to download for offline playback.
- Your app preferences (equalizer, caching, theme, lyrics sources, and related settings).

This information remains on your device except when communicating with the services described below.

---

## 3. Network Connections

The app connects only to:

- Your Plex Media Server to browse and play your library.
- plex.tv to authenticate your Plex account and retrieve account information.
- Lyrics providers you configure manually.

We do not control these services. Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. iCloud Backup

If you enable iCloud Backup, playlists, ratings, custom artwork references, and app preferences may be synchronized through Apple's CloudKit service.

This information is stored in your personal iCloud account.

The developer does not have access to this data and cannot view its contents.

---

## 5. No Tracking or Advertising

The app contains no:

- Analytics SDKs
- Advertising SDKs
- Tracking SDKs

We do not build a profile of you and we do not share information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 6. Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information.

---

## 7. Your Authentication Token

Your Plex authentication token is stored locally on your device and is used solely to authenticate requests to your Plex server and plex.tv.

It is never sent to the developer.

---

## 8. Your Controls

You remain in control of your data at all times.

You may:

- Clear cached music, images, and lyrics.
- Remove downloaded content.
- Sign out of your Plex account.
- Delete the app and all associated local data.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Because the app collects no personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves.

Material changes will be reflected by updating the date shown at the top of this document.
